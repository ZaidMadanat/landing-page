Welcome to the philosophy club!

Philosophy can be split into different periods including Ancient, Medieval, Renaissance, Modern, Contemporary, and Post-Modern. Each period offers a wide variety of ideas, that are intersting and reflect that period's culture and ideals. Here we will focus on Modern philosophers.

Notable Modern Philosophers
Friedirch Nietzsche's evocative style and provactive ideas generates passion for readers. A lot of his works focused on nihilism.
Arthur Schopenhauer popularised indian philosophy ideas in the west like asceticism, denail of self, and the notion of the world-as-appearance
Immanuel Kant's argued that all experiences and objects of experience are mere "appearances" He has influenced many modern works of metaphysics, ethics and aesthetics.
Georg Hegel's system is divided into three parts: science of logic, philosophy of nature, and the philosophy of spirit.
